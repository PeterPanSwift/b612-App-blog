  • 方法 2: 在 prompt 輸入指令。

範例: 使用 AI 創作三隻小豬的圖文繪本。

prompt: 請設計搭配插畫的 three pigs 英文故事，描述每一頁的文字內容跟插畫描述。

請 AI 依據每一頁的插畫描述畫圖，以下為 page 1、page 2、page 3 的插圖。

prompt: 請繪製 Three little pigs standing outside their mother’s cozy cottage, waving goodbye to her. The first pig is holding a bundle of straw, the second pig has a stack of sticks, and the third pig carries a blueprint and a construction hat.

prompt: 請繪製 The first pig, giggling, is throwing bundles of straw together to make a simple, wobbly house. He has a kite flying nearby and a big smile on his face.

prompt: 請繪製 The second pig, sweating a bit, is hammering sticks together. The house looks a bit crooked but more stable than the straw one. He has a cup of lemonade ready to celebrate his “hard work.”

  • App 搭配的技術。

SwiftUI 可用 TabView 搭配 page 樣式實現，UIKit 可用 UIPageViewController 或 UICollectionView 或 UIScrollView 或 segue 實現，Flutter 可用 PageView 實現。
