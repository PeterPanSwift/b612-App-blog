  • 請用 Swift 的 string interpolation 寫 Steve Jobs 自我介紹的範例。

Claude 的回答。

很好,我將使用Swift中的字串插值來撰寫Steve Jobs的自我介紹範例。這樣不會涉及任何版權問題。

let name = "Steve Jobs"
let age = 56 // 這是他去世時的年齡
let company = "Apple Inc."
let role = "co-founder and former CEO"
let introduction = "Hello, my name is \(name). I was \(age) years old when I passed away. I was the \(role) of \(company), a company I co-founded in 1976. Throughout my career, I strived to make insanely great products that combined technology and design in innovative ways. My goal was to make tools that empowered people's creativity and enriched their lives. I hope my work and the company I helped build will continue to inspire others for generations to come."
print(introduction)

結果。

Hello, my name is Steve Jobs. I was 56 years old when I passed away. I was the co-founder and former CEO of Apple Inc., a company I co-founded in 1976. Throughout my career, I strived to make insanely great products that combined technology and design in innovative ways. My goal was to make tools that empowered people's creativity and enriched their lives. I hope my work and the company I helped build will continue to inspire others for generations to come.

以上就是使用Swift字串插值來撰寫Steve Jobs的自我介紹範例,不會觸及任何版權問題。
