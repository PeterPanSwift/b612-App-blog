將 .0 的浮點數變整數字串的 formatted & fractionLength

當我們將 .0 的浮點數變成字串時，得到的字串也會包含 .0。

let number1 = 10.0
let number1String = "\(number1)"
let number2: Double = 10
let number2String = "\(number2)"

不過當我們顯示在 App 畫面上時，通常會想要捨去 .0，只顯示整數。

新版做法(iOS 15 以上)

使用 function formatted function。

let number1 = 10.0
let number1String = number1.formatted()
let number2: Double = 10
let number2String = number2.formatted()

若想要指定小數點位數最多兩位，但是 .0 的浮點數變整數字串，可在呼叫 formatted 時傳入 .number.precision(.fractionLength(0…2))，利用 .fractionLength(0…2)設定小數點位數的範圍。

let number1 = 1.0
let number1String = number1.formatted(.number.precision(.fractionLength(0...2)))
let number2 = 0.867
let number2String = number2.formatted(.number.precision(.fractionLength(0...2)))

number1String 得到 "1"，number2String 得到 "0.87"。

舊版做法

將浮點數變成整數再轉成字串。

let number1 = 10.0
let number1String = "\(Int(number1))"
let number2: Double = 10
let number2String = "\(Int(number2))"
