請 AI 朗讀英文故事和創作英文繪本學英文

最近彼得潘在程式家教時跟學生聊到小朋友學英文。小朋友通常都喜歡聽故事，如果家長可以唸英文故事給小朋友聽應該會有不錯的學習效果。

然而許多家長心有餘而力不足，因為他沒時間或沒自信唸英文。不過沒關係，現在有萬能的 AI 可以幫家長代勞，以下彼得潘以三隻小豬的故事示範如何請 AI 用接近真人的聲音說英文故事和創作英文繪本。(使用 GPT)

請 AI 朗讀英文故事

prompt: tell me story Three Little Pigs。

點選下圖的喇叭按鈕，請 AI 以接近真人的聲音朗讀故事。

GPT 內建許多不錯的聲音，包含各種男生女生的聲音，我們可選擇喜歡的聲音朗讀故事。

看不懂 AI 寫的英文故事時，也可以請 AI 提供中英對照。

prompt: 寫中文翻譯，英文一句，中文一句。

Once upon a time, three little pigs lived with their mother.  
從前，有三隻小豬和牠們的媽媽住在一起。

One day, their mother said, "You are all grown up now and must build your own houses. Be careful of the big bad wolf!"  
有一天，媽媽說：「你們都長大了，該去建自己的房子了。要小心那隻大野狼！」

The first little pig, who was a bit lazy, decided to build his house out of straw.  
第一隻小豬有點懶惰，決定用稻草蓋房子。

It was quick and easy, and he could play all day.  
這樣蓋房子又快又簡單，然後他就可以整天玩耍了。

The second little pig, who was also not a hard worker, chose to build his house out of sticks.  
第二隻小豬也不愛努力工作，選擇用木棍蓋房子。

It wasn’t very strong, but he, too, finished fast and ran off to play with his brother.  
雖然不太堅固，但他很快就蓋好了，也跑去跟哥哥玩了。

But the third little pig was hardworking and smart.  
然而，第三隻小豬既勤勞又聰明。

He decided to build his house out of bricks.  
他決定用磚頭蓋房子。

He worked for days and days, making sure his house was strong and safe.  
他花了好幾天，確保房子堅固又安全。

Then came the big, bad wolf!  
大野狼來了！

He was hungry and looking for some piggy snacks.  
他又餓又想吃點小豬點心。

He went to the first little pig’s house and said:  
他來到第一隻小豬的房子前，說：

"Little pig, little pig, let me come in!"  
「小豬，小豬，讓我進來！」

The pig squealed, "Not by the hair of my chinny chin chin!"  
小豬尖叫道：「才不讓你進來呢！」

"Then I’ll huff and I’ll puff, and I’ll blow your house in!" roared the wolf.  
大野狼吼道：「那我就用力吹，用力呼，把你的房子吹垮！」

He huffed, and he puffed, and the straw house came crashing down.  
他用力吹啊吹，稻草房子就倒了。

The first little pig ran to his brother's stick house for safety.  
第一隻小豬跑到第二隻小豬的木棍房子裡尋求安全。

The wolf followed and knocked on the second pig’s door.  
大野狼跟著來敲第二隻小豬的門。

"Little pigs, little pigs, let me come in!"  
「小豬們，小豬們，讓我進來！」

The pigs cried, "Not by the hair of our chinny chin chins!"  
小豬們大叫：「才不讓你進來呢！」

"Then I’ll huff and I’ll puff, and I’ll blow your house in!" the wolf threatened.  
大野狼威脅道：「那我就用力吹，用力呼，把你們的房子吹垮！」

The wolf huffed, and he puffed, and the stick house fell to pieces.  
大野狼吹啊吹，木棍房子也垮了。

The two pigs ran as fast as they could to their brother’s brick house.  
兩隻小豬拼命跑到第三隻小豬的磚頭房子裡。

The wolf, getting hungrier and angrier, followed them to the brick house.  
大野狼越來越餓，越來越生氣，跟著跑到磚頭房子前。

He banged on the door and said:  
他猛敲門，說：

"Little pigs, little pigs, let me come in!"  
「小豬們，小豬們，讓我進來！」

The pigs bravely replied, "Not by the hair of our chinny chin chins!"  
小豬們勇敢地回答：「才不讓你進來呢！」

"Then I’ll huff and I’ll puff, and I’ll blow your house in!" shouted the wolf.  
大野狼大吼：「那我就用力吹，用力呼，把你們的房子吹垮！」

The wolf huffed and puffed, and he puffed and huffed, but no matter how hard he tried, the brick house stood firm.  
大野狼用力吹啊吹，呼啊呼，但無論怎麼用力，磚頭房子都穩穩地站著。

The wolf was exhausted and out of breath.  
大野狼累得筋疲力盡，上氣不接下氣。

He decided to come up with a sneaky plan and climbed up onto the roof to slide down the chimney.  
他決定使出詭計，爬上屋頂，準備從煙囪滑下去。

But the third little pig was ready.  
但第三隻小豬早有準備。

He had a big pot of boiling water waiting in the fireplace.  
他在壁爐裡燒了一大鍋滾燙的水。

As the wolf slid down the chimney, he fell right into the boiling water with a yelp and shot back up, running far away into the forest, never to bother the pigs again.  
當大野狼從煙囪滑下來時，掉進了滾燙的水裡，慘叫一聲，急忙跳出來，跑進森林，再也不敢來煩小豬們了。

The three little pigs celebrated their safety.  
三隻小豬慶祝終於安全了。

The first two pigs learned an important lesson about hard work and promised to be more like their wise brother.  
前兩隻小豬學到了一個重要的教訓，答應以後要像聰明的弟弟一樣勤奮。

And they all lived happily ever after!  
他們從此過著幸福快樂的生活！

請 AI 創作英文繪本

我們也可以請 AI 創作英文繪本，搭配生動有趣的插圖吸引小朋友的注意力。

prompt: 請設計搭配插畫的 three pigs 英文故事，描述每一頁的文字內容跟插畫描述。

請 AI 依據每一頁的插畫描述畫圖，以下為 page 1、page 2、page 3 的插圖。

  • 請繪製 Three little pigs standing outside their mother’s cozy cottage, waving goodbye to her. The first pig is holding a bundle of straw, the second pig has a stack of sticks, and the third pig carries a blueprint and a construction hat.
  • 請繪製 The first pig, giggling, is throwing bundles of straw together to make a simple, wobbly house. He has a kite flying nearby and a big smile on his face.
  • 請繪製 The second pig, sweating a bit, is hammering sticks together. The house looks a bit crooked but more stable than the straw one. He has a cup of lemonade ready to celebrate his “hard work.”
