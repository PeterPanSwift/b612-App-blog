顯示多個 SwiftUI preview

從 SwiftUI preview 預覽 App 畫面時，有時我們會想預覽畫面在不同情況呈現的樣子，此時我們可創建多個 preview，然後在不同的 preview 分頁間切換顯示。

以下我們以 PostRow 為例，目標創作兩個 preview，分別顯示鞦韆上和滑梯上用 MacBook 寫程式的可愛男孩(ps: 感謝 AI 繪製圖片)。

struct PostRow: View {
    let imageName: String
    let content: String
    
    var body: some View {
        HStack {
            Image(imageName)
                .resizable()
                .scaledToFill()
                .frame(width: 100, height: 100)
                .clipShape(.circle)
            Text(content)
        }
    }
}

顯示多個 SwiftUI preview

一個 #Preview 可建立一個 preview，2 個 #Preview 可建立兩個 preview，建立幾個 #Preview 就可創建幾個 preview 分頁。

#Preview {
    PostRow(imageName: "swing", content: "A cute boy with a delightful expression, of Caucasian descent, casually dressed, sitting on a swing and working on a MacBook with a visible Apple logo. The setting is a bright park with green trees and grass in the background. The boy has curly blonde hair, and is engrossed in his laptop screen, which displays lines of code, with sunlight filtering through the leaves above.")
}

#Preview {
    PostRow(imageName: "slide", content: "A cute boy of South Asian descent with a playful smile, sitting at the top of a slide in a children's playground, using a MacBook on his lap. He is wearing bright, comfortable play clothes and sneakers, and he is surrounded by vibrant greenery. His attention is dedicated to the screen as he programs.")
}

兩個 #Preview 實現 2 個 preview 分頁，preview 上的標題代表 #Preview 的行數。

設定 preview 的名字

如果想更清楚辨別 preview 分頁，建立 #Preview 時可設定分頁的標題，以下我們分別設定 swing 和 slide。

#Preview("swing") {
    PostRow(imageName: "swing", content: "A cute boy with a delightful expression, of Caucasian descent, casually dressed, sitting on a swing and working on a MacBook with a visible Apple logo. The setting is a bright park with green trees and grass in the background. The boy has curly blonde hair, and is engrossed in his laptop screen, which displays lines of code, with sunlight filtering through the leaves above.")
}

#Preview("slide") {
    PostRow(imageName: "slide", content: "A cute boy of South Asian descent with a playful smile, sitting at the top of a slide in a children's playground, using a MacBook on his lap. He is wearing bright, comfortable play clothes and sneakers, and he is surrounded by vibrant greenery. His attention is dedicated to the screen as he programs.")
}

用 Group 包含多個 view

如果懶得切換 preview 分頁，我們也可用 Group 包含多個 view，一次觀看多個 view 呈現的樣子。

#Preview {
    Group {
        PostRow(imageName: "swing", content: "A cute boy with a delightful expression, of Caucasian descent, casually dressed, sitting on a swing and working on a MacBook with a visible Apple logo. The setting is a bright park with green trees and grass in the background. The boy has curly blonde hair, and is engrossed in his laptop screen, which displays lines of code, with sunlight filtering through the leaves above.")
        PostRow(imageName: "slide", content: "A cute boy of South Asian descent with a playful smile, sitting at the top of a slide in a children's playground, using a MacBook on his lap. He is wearing bright, comfortable play clothes and sneakers, and he is surrounded by vibrant greenery. His attention is dedicated to the screen as he programs.")
    }
}
